# About

Holy Quran is your everyday companion for reading the Quran, listening to
master reciters, knowing prayer times, daily azkar, tasbih, qibla direction,
khatma tracking, and personal reminders.

## Content sources

- **Quranic text**: Madani Mushaf (King Fahd Complex edition).
- **Recitations**: EveryAyah.com and master reciters.
- **Fonts**: QPC V1 from the King Fahd Complex.
- **Adhan audio**: Internet Archive and Assabile (open-license).
